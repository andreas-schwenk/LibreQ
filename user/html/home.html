LibreQ

LibreQ is a free and flexible toolkit for organizing, tagging, and sharing Moodle questions across departments or entire schools. It supports collaborative development of high quality educational content while allowing each institution to define its own structure and workflows.

Teachers and subject coordinators can build shared question collections, apply custom classifications, and make selected content available for others to use. All data remains under your control and can be tailored to meet your curriculum or assessment needs.

This platform is designed to support open education and long term reuse of digital learning materials.
